The Workshops

Learn essential data-skills such as finding, scraping, cleaning, analysing, and visualising data and apply you newly-developed skills in the Story Hunt!

We offer 5 weekly workshops, each 2 hours, starting on 16 May 2017.

1. Getting Started

16 May 2017

The first meeting will be an introduction in the coming weeks topics. We will present inspiring data journalism stories and give an introduction of large data projects at OKFDE such as subsidystories.eu and JedeSchule.de.

Speaker: Anna Alberts, Helene Hahn

2. Finding and Getting the Data

23 May 2017

In this workshop we will start with the basics of data. We put questions to datasets and discuss effective ways to find data, how to get to know your data and spreadsheet tips and tricks.

Speaker: Anna Alberts, Helene Hahn

3. Scraping & Cleaning Data

30 May 2017

In this workshop we explain the basics of scraping and show you meaningful ways and tools to obtain data from the web. In most cases, datasets are messy and need to be cleaned in some ways. Tools such as OpenRefine and Excel provide great ways to produce clean data.

Speaker: Moritz Neujeffski, Michael Peters, Knut Perseke

4. Analytics: Basic Intro into Statistics & Spreadsheets

13 June 2017

Statistics have a bad reputation of being hard, and manipulative. In this workshop we will show why you should use statistics and how you can use basic statistics to validate your data, understand it and run basic functions for first analysis.

Speaker: Michael Peters, Knut Perseke

5. Visualising Data

20 June 2017

Data comes to life in visualisations. In this workshop we will discuss ground rules of design and data visualisations. We will work on telling a story with data.

Speaker: Lisa Rost
