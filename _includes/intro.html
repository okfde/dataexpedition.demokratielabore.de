What is 'The Story Hunt'?

'The Story Hunt: Uncover the EU' is a programme hosted by Datenschule and OpenBudgets of Open Knowledge Foundation Germany. Together with journalists, domain-experts, developers and designers , we want to develop and practice the skills needed to hunt stories in financial data.

The programme is split into two separate parts: a series of Trainings and a weekend-long Data Expedition in a forgotten castle outside of Berlin.

The bootcamp is tailored to aspiring data journalists that are interested in improving their data skills. The trainings will culminate in the workshop at end of June, where - together with proven experts - we are going to dive into a massive database of the European Unionâ€™s primary financial instrument, the ESIFunds. During the expedition we will form interdisciplinary teams that collaboratively work on finding stories, leads, and doing analysis on data around the EU and its money flows. This will offer an ideal opportunity to practice the acquired skills in a supportive environment.
