Workshops

The workshops are planned to accommodate up to 10 people. Our aim is to put together a group diverse in background but with a shared first experience in working with data.

Requirement: Commitment for 5 weekly meetings, starting the 23 May 2017 and participation in the (free) Data Expedition 30 June - 2nd July. You should have affinity for data or interest in EU topics, plus a ton of Enthusiasm! :-)

To apply, click the button below and state your motivation including, if you already have one, a first idea of what you would like to investigate using the data on subsidystories.eu
