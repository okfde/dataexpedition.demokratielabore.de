Trainings

The Story Hunt Training sessions are the preparation series for participating in our weekend workshop. If you are based in Berlin and an early-career data journalists, EU policy analyst, or NGO or advocacy officer, we invite you to sign up for our 5 training sessions in May and June, where you learn the basics of data-gathering, cleaning and analysis in the realm of EU policy. We offer 5 weekly 2 hour training sessions teaching essential data skills: the basics of data-journalism, obtaining and cleaning data, as well as analysing and visualising it. The trainings will take place at the Open Knowledge Foundation Germany’s offices in Berlin.

The trainings offered cover the basic skills of finding, scraping, analysing, and visualising data. The Bootcamp will take place in the offices of OKF DE in Berlin.
