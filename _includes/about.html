About

The EU and its money flows are notoriously complex and partially intransparent. Over the past year, the team behind SubsidyStories.eu has gathered, cleaned, and concatenated data on the EU’s most important financial instrument: the European Structural Investment Funds (ESIF). Together with representatives of different disciplines (journalists, developers, domain experts, and designers) we want to immerse ourselves in this data, hunting for stories.

That is why the Open Knowledge Foundation Germany has worked over the past year to open the data on EU subsidies with subsidystories.eu. The work has shown that there is a great discrepancy between the data quality published by the EU member states, enshrouding possible misues in a cloud of inaccessiblity. You can view a report on the data here.

This is only the beginning of our EU Transparency work and we invite the participants to work with this data together with us on analysing money flows and increasing EU Transparency.

Together with the teams of Datenschule and OpenBudgets.eu, we will host a data expedition. On this three day workshop, the Bootcamp’s participants will work together with 25 experts from the field of journalism, programming, advocacy, and design to work on data, analysis and story-telling.
